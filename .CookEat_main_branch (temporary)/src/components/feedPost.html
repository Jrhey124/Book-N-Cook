Try my recipe for Pares, a delicious Filipino dish!

Ingredients:

  • Beef Shank
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Onions
  • Soy Sauce
  • Star Anise
  • Rice

Instructions:

  1. Brown the beef shank in a pot.
  2. Simmer with garlic, ginger, onions, soy sauce, and star anise for 2 hours.
  3. Cook the rice and serve it with the beef stew.
  4. Enjoy your delicious Pares!
